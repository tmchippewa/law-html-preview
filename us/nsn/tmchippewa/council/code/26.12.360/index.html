Turtle Mountain Band of Chippewa Indians Tribal Code.

26.12.360 Failure to Provide Information

Any person who is required to provide information under Title 42, the Sex Offender Registration Code, based on any conviction and violates that section shall be guilty of a Class [4] four offense.

(TMBC1246-06-18; TMBC686-01-12.)
