Turtle Mountain Band of Chippewa Indians Tribal Code.

26.11.050 Cruelty to Livestock

(a) Any person(s)shall be guilty of a Class [3] three offense who does not supply the livestock animal a sufficient quantity of wholesome food and water when causing or confining the livestock animal.

(b) This section also provides that any officer of the department of health, environmental control, animal control or law enforcement may lawfully destroy, or cause to be destroyed, any animal found abandoned and not properly cared for, appearing to be infectiously diseased, injured past recovery for any useful purpose.

(TMBC1246-06-18.)

Prior Codifications

§26.11.050 was formerly codified as 26.1104.01
