Turtle Mountain Band of Chippewa Indians Tribal Code.

26.13.090 Assault Resulting in Serious Bodily Injury

(a) It shall be unlawful to assault another person and inflict serious bodily injury and a Class Five (5) offense.

(b) An assault that causes serious injury that creates a substantial risk of death, or that causes serious permanent disfigurement, coma, a permanent or protracted condition that causes extreme pain, or permanent or protracted loss or impairment of the function of any bodily member or organ, or that results in prolonged hospitalization.

(TMBC 219-03-23; TMBC 1246-06-18; TMBC 5619-04-94.)

Prior Codifications
