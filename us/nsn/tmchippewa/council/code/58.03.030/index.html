Turtle Mountain Band of Chippewa Indians Tribal Code.

58.03.030 Food Additives

(a) Added Flavors. The label of a Class 2 or 3 food product to which spices or other natural flavors are added shall declare such flavors in the list of ingredients, as required, in order by weight, with the largest quantity listed first. (Please refer to food code policy and regulations for more information).
