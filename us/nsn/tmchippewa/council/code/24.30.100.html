Turtle Mountain Band of Chippewa Indians Tribal Code.

24.30.100 Warranties

(a) If its presentation is honored, the beneficiary warrants:

(1) To the issuer, any other person to whom presentation is made, and the applicant that there is no fraud or forgery of the kind described in subsection (a) of Section 24.30.090; and

(2) To the applicant that the drawing does not violate any agreement between the applicant and beneficiary or any other agreement intended by them to be augmented by the letter of credit.

(b) The warranties in subsection 1 are in addition to warranties arising under Chapter 24.14 – Chapter 24.19, Chapter 24.20 – Chapter 24.24, Chapter 24.32 – Chapter 24.37, and Chapter 24.38 – Chapter 24.48 because of the presentation or transfer of documents covered by any of those chapters.

(TMBC 1150-08-20.)
