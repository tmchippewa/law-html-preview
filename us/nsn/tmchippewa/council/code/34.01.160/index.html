Turtle Mountain Band of Chippewa Indians Tribal Code.

34.01.160 Interest; Ceases at Maturity Unless Presented for Payment

All bonds issued pursuant to the provisions of this Bond Code must contain a provision that interest thereon ceases at maturity unless the holder thereof presents the same for payment and payment is refused.
