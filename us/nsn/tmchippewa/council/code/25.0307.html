25.0307 Conduct of Class Iii Games of Chance

Until such time as additional regulations are deemed necessary by the Tribal Gaming Commission, the conduct of all Class III games shall be governed by those applicable provisions of the Tribal-State Gaming Compact referred to in Section 25.0302.

1. Employee Gambling Policy. The following is the policy of the Turtle Mountain Chippewa Casino authorizing employees to play games of chance.

a. Purpose. To inform the employees of the Sky Dancer Hotel & Casino the games of chance authorized to be played by employees for the purpose of upholding the integrity of the operation and to prevent an appearance of possible collusion.

(1) Employees shall not participate in any promotion, play progressive slot machines, or tournaments when the Casino contributes to the prize money. No employee may possess a player's club card.

(2) Employees will be allowed to gamble before or after their scheduled shift.

(3) No employee shall gamble in the casino while in uniform or wear a jacket to cover their uniform.

(4) No Gaming Management employee, Accounting, MIS, Surveillance, Marketing Director, or Tribal Gaming employee shall be allowed to gamble.

b. Table games/poker. All dealers shall be allowed to play table games including poker with the exception of Managers and Assistant Managers.

c. Slot machines. Employees within the drop department and slot departments, excluding attendants, shall not be allowed to play any slot machines.

d. Bingo. No employee of this department, including Snack Shack employees, shall be allowed to play bingo.

e. Pull tabs. No Pull Tab or Main Bank employee shall be allowed to play pull tabs.

f. Simulcast. No Simulcast employee shall be allowed to participate in any type of betting in this area.

Any questions in reference to this policy shall be referred to the Gaming Managers or the Players Club Department.
