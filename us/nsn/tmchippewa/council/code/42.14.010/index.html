Turtle Mountain Band of Chippewa Indians Tribal Code.

42.14.010 Housing Policies

Eligibility and Admission Rules. Rule #2 Ineligibility for Admission (pg. 13 of 14 letters F&G)

(a) Ineligibility for Admission.

Applicants and their household composition member(s) will be considered ineligible for the rental program if there is:

(1) An incomplete application or an application with false information;

(2) An outstanding debt with the Housing Authority, owned by the applicant and/or the household composition member(s). excluding an elderly applicant who has entered into a repayment agreement;

(3) Any member of the household has a conviction of drug-related criminal activity unless successful completion of a certified rehabilitation program (Tribal or State recognized);

(4) Any member of the household convicted of violent criminal activity, within the past three (3) years;

(5) Any member of the household convicted of gang activity or gang related activity, within the past three (3) years;

(6) Any member of the household is subject to a life time requirement under a sex offender registration program, who has registered after December 11, 2003;

(7) Any member of the household is currently under a sex offender registration program that is less than a lifetime registration requirement, who has registered after December 11, 2003;

(8) Any household member's abuse or pattern of abuse of alcohol interferes with the health, safety or right to peaceful enjoyment of the premises by other residents.

(b) If an applicant is placed on the waiting list and it is found that circumstances have changed and the applicant is no longer eligible for admission based upon the above reasons, they will be notified in writing and will be removed from the waiting list.
