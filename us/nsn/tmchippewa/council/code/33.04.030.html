Turtle Mountain Band of Chippewa Indians Tribal Code.

33.04.030 Maintenance of Records

The Director shall maintain a record of all applications and all Certificates of Titled issued by the Director:

(a) According to the title number.

(b) Alphabetically, according to the name of owner.

(c) In a fire proofed type of vault.

(d) In any manner which the Director shall desire.

Prior Codifications

§33.04.030 was formerly codified as 33.0403
