Title 20 Recreational Development Authority

Chapter 20.01 Purpose

20.01.010 Purpose

The Authority is created and established to operate, manage, maintain and carry out all of the responsibilities, obligations, and privileges, of the Turtle Mountain Band of Chippewa Indians in respect to the Recreational Development of Tribal owned or leased land on the Turtle Mountain Indian Reservation.

Prior Codifications

§20.01.010 was formerly codified as 20.0101

Chapter 20.02 Organization

20.02.010 Management of Authority and Appointment of Board Members

The affairs of the Turtle Mountain Recreational Development Authority shall be managed by a seven (7) member Board of Commissioners composed of persons appointed by the Tribal Council of the Turtle Mountain Band of Chippewa Indians.

Prior Codifications

§20.02.010 was formerly codified as 20.0201

20.02.020 Composition and Terms of Original Board of Commissioners

The term of office of the Board members appointed at the expiration of each regular term shall be six (6) years.

Prior Codifications

§20.02.020 was formerly codified as 20.0202

20.02.030 Terms of Board Members After Original Board

The term of office of the Board members appointed at the expiration of each regular term shall be six (6) years.

Prior Codifications

§20.02.030 was formerly codified as 20.0203

20.02.040 Officers of Board of Commissioners

The officers of the Board shall be a Chairman, a Vice-Chairman and a Secretary-Treasurer elected by the members of the Board at their first meeting. The Tribal Council appoints new members each year in September of each odd numbered year.

Prior Codifications

§20.02.040 was formerly codified as 20.0204

20.02.050 Compensation and Reimbursement of Board Members

The Board members shall receive compensation for their services, as established by the Board, and may be entitled to reimbursement, as established by the Tribal Council, for expenses, including traveling expenses if any incurred in the discharge of their Authority duties. All Board members authorized to handle funds shall be bonded to the Tribal Council in the amount of five thousand dollars ($5,000).

Prior Codifications

§20.02.050 was formerly codified as 20.0105

20.02.060 Quorum Required for Transaction of Business

Four (4) members of the Board shall constitute a quorum for the transaction of Authority business.

Prior Codifications

§20.02.060 was formerly codified as 20.0206

20.02.070 Duties of the Secretary-Treasurer

The Secretary-Treasurer of the Board shall keep complete and accurate records of all meetings and action taken by the Board, and shall keep full and accurate financial records, and make quarterly reports to the Board and to the Tribal Council, and submit a yearly audit by a public accountant to the Tribal Council.

Prior Codifications

§20.02.070 was formerly codified as 20.0207

20.02.080 Authority of Board

The Board shall have authority to exercise, by majority vote of those present and voting, any and all powers delegated to the Authority pursuant to Provision III below.

Prior Codifications

§20.02.080 was formerly codified as 20.0208

20.02.090 Meetings; Who to Call and When

Meetings of the Board shall be held as the Authority business warrants, and upon call of the Chairman of the Board. The Board shall meet at least every three (3) months in any event.

Chapter 20.03 Powers

20.03.010 Powers

The Authority shall have the following powers, which it may exercise through its Board of Commissioners, consistent with the purposes for which the Authority is established:

(a) To enter into agreements, contracts and understandings with any governmental agency, Federal, State, or local including the Tribal Council of the Turtle Mountain Band of Chippewa Indians or with any person, partnership, corporation or Indian Tribe, and to agree with any conditions attached to Federal financial assistance consistent with the purposes of such authority as described herein.

(b) To take whatever steps it deems necessary to manage, operate, and maintain the buildings, and the realty on which said buildings are situated, and to institute, operate, manage and conduct recreational enterprises on the Turtle Mountain Indian Reservation.

(c) To enter into and execute long-term leases, as lessee, with the Turtle Mountain Band of Chippewa Indians or Bureau of Indian Affairs, or individual owners, as lessor, covering any realty located on the Turtle Mountain Indian Reservation, or other Tribal-owned lands.

(d) To hold, manage, and to rent or sublet, all or any part of the space of buildings or realty under their control, and to establish such rules and regulations concerning the rental, subletting, and use of all or any part of the buildings or realty leased.

(e) To execute mortgages of leasehold interests created pursuant to Subsection (c) above, and to offer said mortgages of the leasehold interests to any Federal agency, or other lender as security for loan funds obtained from any Federal agency, or other lender in the financing of Recreational Development projects.

(f) To maintain accounts in any National or State Bank in North Dakota for any Recreational Developments, and any other income derived from the use of the buildings or realty shall be deposited intact to the same bank accounts. All disbursements therefrom, including the monthly installment payments to the Federal agencies or other lenders to repay loans, shall be paid out of the aforesaid bank accounts by check or checks signed by the Chairman and the Secretary-Treasurer of the Board.

(g) To carry out all the obligations, and to protect all the interests of the Turtle Mountain Band of Chippewa Indians in and to the provisions of all contracts and agreements hereafter executed by the Tribal Council of the said Tribe concerning the Recreational Development projects.

Prior Codifications

§20.03.010 was formerly codified as 20.0301

Chapter 20.04 Dissolution

20.04.010 Dissolution

The Authority shall not be dissolved for a period of fifty (50) years, or until such time as the mortgages of the leasehold interests executed pursuant to Provision III(e) have been satisfied in full, or until such time as any loans executed pursuant to Provision II(c) have expired.

Prior Codifications

§20.04.010 was formerly codified as 20.0401
